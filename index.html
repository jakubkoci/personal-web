About

I'm a product-minded software engineer and craftsman. My passion is building digital products. I have various experiences across the stack, from backend development over web and mobile frontend development to UX/UI design.

My current area of interest are decentralized technologies having a strong expertise in self-sovereign identity.

I write code mainly in TypeScript/JavaScript (React/React Native, Node.js). I also have extensive experience with Java. I learned functional programming with Clojure and I've played around a bit with Rust. I strive for simplicity and application of best practices like clean code, TDD, and DevOps. I love learning and learning by teaching. I'm a co-host of a podcast about software craftsmanship (in Czech). I was a co-organizer and mentor at several coderetreats.

I spent the vast majority of my career in FinTech, building and maintaining a backend application for the loan approval process, creating an extensive client-side application for personal finance management from scratch, and now building a mobile app and framework for self-sovereign identity.
